## The eFoil Decision: Rent or Buy?

eFoils are not cheap. A premium board like the Audi e-tron eFoil costs $10,000–$15,000, and that is before you account for accessories, maintenance, transport, and storage. For many people, renting is the smarter choice — but for others, ownership makes sense.

This guide breaks down the real numbers and practical considerations.

## The True Cost of Owning an eFoil

### Purchase Price
- **Premium boards** (Audi e-tron, Fliteboard, Lift): $10,000–$15,000
- **Mid-range boards**: $6,000–$9,000
- **Budget/used boards**: $3,000–$5,000

### Ongoing Costs
- **Battery replacement:** $1,500–$3,000 every 2–3 years (lithium batteries degrade with use and age)
- **Propeller and motor service:** $200–$500 annually
- **Wing and mast maintenance:** $100–$300 annually
- **Charger and electrical:** $50–$100 if replacement needed
- **Transport:** Roof rack, vehicle modifications, or shipping costs for travel
- **Storage:** Climate-controlled storage recommended for batteries (if you do not ride year-round)
- **Insurance:** $300–$800 annually for watercraft insurance

### Estimated Annual Cost of Ownership
For a premium board ridden 50 sessions per year:

| Item | Annual Cost |
|---|---|
| Depreciation (5-year life) | $2,000–$3,000 |
| Battery degradation | $500–$1,000 |
| Maintenance | $300–$500 |
| Insurance | $300–$800 |
| Transport/storage | $200–$600 |
| **Total** | **$3,300–$5,900** |

That works out to $66–$118 per session if you ride 50 times a year.

## When Renting Makes More Sense

### You ride fewer than 30 times per year
If you are riding less than once a week, the per-session cost of ownership exceeds rental rates in most markets.

### You travel frequently
Transporting an eFoil by air is expensive (oversize luggage + lithium battery regulations) and often impractical. Renting at your destination eliminates this entirely.

### You want to try before you commit
Spending $250 on a rental is a low-risk way to discover whether you enjoy eFoiling before investing $12,000.

### You are on holiday
If you are visiting the Maldives on a yacht charter or resort stay, renting is the obvious choice. We [deliver to your location](/yachts) — no transport or logistics needed.

### You want premium equipment without capital outlay
Renting gives you access to top-tier boards (Audi e-tron, Fliteboard Ultra) that you might not purchase outright. Equipment is professionally maintained between sessions.

## When Buying Makes More Sense

### You ride 50+ times per year
At high session counts, the per-session cost of ownership drops below typical rental rates. If you live near water and can ride regularly, owning pays off.

### You live in a location with no rental operators
If there are no eFoil rental services in your area, buying is your only option for regular riding.

### You want to customise your setup
Owners can choose specific wing sizes, mast lengths, and board configurations to match their riding style. Rental operators typically offer one standard setup.

### You want to start a business
If you plan to offer eFoil experiences commercially, purchasing equipment is necessary. Our [partner operator programme](/partners) provides equipment, training, and booking infrastructure to help you get started.

## A Hybrid Approach

Many riders adopt a hybrid strategy:
1. **Rent first** to learn and decide if eFoiling is for them.
2. **Buy for home use** if they ride frequently in their local area.
3. **Continue renting while travelling** to avoid transport hassles.

This approach gives you the best of both worlds — ownership benefits where it makes sense, rental convenience everywhere else.
