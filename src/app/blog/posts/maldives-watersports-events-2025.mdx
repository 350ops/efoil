## Maldives Competitive Watersports Calendar

Maldives hosts a growing number of international and local watersports events each year. From the world's most luxurious surf contest to community kite festivals, here's what's on the calendar.

---

## Surf Competitions

### Four Seasons Surfing Champions Trophy

The crown jewel of Maldivian surf events. Held at **Kuda Huraa** in **North Male' Atoll** every **August**, this invitation-only contest brings legends of the sport to compete in distinctive single-fin, twin-fin, and thruster divisions. Hosted by the Four Seasons Resort, it's arguably the world's most luxurious surfing competition.

- **When:** August (recurring annual event)
- **Where:** Kuda Huraa, North Male' Atoll
- **Format:** Invitation-only, multi-division (single-fin, twin-fin, thruster)
- **Status:** Confirmed recurring

### Asian Surfing Championships

A major regional qualifier for global events, held at **Thulusdhoo's** famous **Cokes break** — one of Maldives' fastest hollow waves. High-performance shortboard surfing at the heart of Maldivian surf culture.

- **When:** July (varies by year)
- **Where:** Thulusdhoo, Cokes Break
- **Format:** Open entry, competitive shortboard
- **Status:** Varies annually

### Soneva Surf Pro

An exclusive invitational at **Baa Atoll** focused on eco-conscious surfing and luxury hospitality. Combines world-class reef break waves with Soneva's sustainability mission.

- **When:** October
- **Where:** Baa Atoll
- **Format:** Invitational
- **Status:** Exclusive annual event

## Bodyboarding

### Visit Maldives Pro

Part of the **IBC World Tour**, this is the premier bodyboarding event in Maldives. Top international athletes compete at the capital's break at **Varunulaa Raalhugan** in **Male' City**. A confirmed annual fixture on the international bodyboarding circuit.

- **When:** July – August
- **Where:** Male' City (Varunulaa Raalhugan)
- **Format:** IBC World Tour stop
- **Status:** Confirmed

---

## Kitesurf & Wind Events

### Kuredu Kite Week

Workshops, coaching sessions, and fun races at one of Maldives' best wind locations. **Lhaviyani Atoll** is known for vast shallow lagoons and consistent cross-shore winds — ideal for improving skills and meeting the local kite community.

- **When:** June
- **Where:** Kuredu, Lhaviyani Atoll
- **Format:** Workshops + fun races
- **Status:** Annual workshop event

### Raalhu Edhuru Kite Fest

A community-driven celebration of wind sports in the southern Male' atolls. Great for experiencing local Maldivian talent and culture. Open to riders of all levels — from beginners to pros.

- **When:** September
- **Where:** Maafushi / Guraidhoo
- **Format:** Community festival
- **Status:** Local annual event

## eFoil Demo Days

### Try Electric Hydrofoil Surfing

No wind needed, no experience required. eFoil demo events run **year-round** at Hulhumalé and select resort islands, organized by foiltribe and eFoil Maldives.

- **When:** Year-round (no wind dependence)
- **Where:** Hulhumalé + rotating resort locations
- **Cost:** Sessions from $150/person including instruction
- **What's included:** Professional instruction, safety gear, drone footage

## Seasonal Planning Tips

Understanding when each sport peaks helps you plan the perfect trip:

| Month | Wind (avg knots) | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| January | 12 | eFoil, Paddleboard |
| February | 10 | eFoil, Snorkeling, Diving |
| March | 8 | eFoil, Paddleboard |
| April | 9 | Beginner Surf, eFoil |
| May | 15 | Wing Foil, Kitesurf, Surf |
| June | 18 | **Kitesurf, Wing Foil** (prime wind) |
| July | 17 | Pro Surf, Kitesurf |
| August | 16 | **Pro Surf (peak swell)**, Kitesurf |
| September | 15 | Wing Foil, Kitesurf, Surf |
| October | 12 | Surf, Wing Foil |
| November | 11 | Fun Surf, eFoil |
| December | 13 | Lagoon Kitesurf, eFoil |

**Pro tip:** August has the best swell-wind intersection but is also the rainiest. For sunnier conditions with reliable wind, aim for **June or late September**.
