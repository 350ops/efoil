## The First eFoil Operators in Maldives

Before anyone in Maldives had seen an eFoil, **foiltribe** was already on the water.

Operating out of Hulhumalé — the reclaimed island just minutes from Velana International Airport — foiltribe became the first company to offer electric hydrofoil surfboard rentals in Maldives. They saw the potential before anyone else: a country surrounded by the most beautiful water on Earth, where a silent, electric-powered board could let anyone fly above it.

## What Is an eFoil?

For the uninitiated: an **eFoil** (electric hydrofoil) is a surfboard with an electric motor and hydrofoil underneath. Once you reach a certain speed, the board lifts completely out of the water and you glide above the surface — silently, effortlessly, and with a sensation unlike anything else in watersports.

The **Audi e-tron eFoil** used by foiltribe and eFoil Maldives is the premium option — carbon fiber construction, a silent electric motor, wireless hand controller for intuitive speed management, and up to 90 minutes of flight time per charge.

No wind. No waves. No experience needed. Just charge the battery, grab the controller, and fly.

## Why Hulhumalé?

Hulhumalé sits at the edge of the Malé atoll, with sheltered lagoons, easy boat access, and proximity to the airport. It's where most airline crew stay during layovers, where many Maldivian locals live, and where the calm, flat water creates ideal conditions for eFoil sessions — especially for first-timers.

foiltribe set up their base here and built a community around the sport. What started as a rental operation has grown into a hub for eFoil culture in Maldives.

## The Partnership: Day Trips + eFoil

foiltribe noticed that many of their customers — particularly airline crew on layover — wanted more than just an eFoil session. They wanted to see dolphins, snorkel pristine reefs, visit a sandbank, and experience Maldives beyond the hotel.

So they partnered with a boat trip operator to create a combined experience: a curated 5-hour day trip departing from Malé that includes dolphin swimming, reef snorkeling at two locations, a private sandbank with lunch, and a sunset cruise — with an optional **Audi e-tron eFoil session** as an add-on.

The eFoil add-on includes:

- Professional instruction tailored to your level
- Drone footage of your ride
- 360-camera footage
- All safety gear

With the day trip, you get an eFoil session **plus** an entire ocean adventure — dolphins, snorkeling, a private sandbank, and a sunset cruise, all in one package. Contact us for current rates.

## The Content Flywheel

One of the most powerful aspects of eFoiling in Maldives is the content it produces. Drone footage of someone flying above crystal-clear turquoise water is, objectively, some of the most shareable watersport content possible.

foiltribe understood this early. By including professional drone and 360-camera footage with every eFoil session, they turned every customer into a content creator. One crew member posts their footage, their colleagues see it and share it, and bookings follow.

This organic, content-driven growth has become the primary engine for both the eFoil business and the day trip partnership.

## What's Next for foiltribe

foiltribe continues to expand across the atolls — working with resorts, yacht charters, and liveaboards to bring eFoil experiences to more locations. Their partnership with eFoil Maldives means that Audi e-tron eFoils can now be delivered anywhere in the country.

The vision is simple: make Maldives the eFoil capital of the world. With the conditions, the community, and the content — they're well on their way.
